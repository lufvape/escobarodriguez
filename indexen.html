About us

We are a specialist firm in legal consulting and counseling, focus in disputes avoidance and conflict management in pursuit of protection economic and social interest of our clients, transaction cost mitigation and efficiency in its operations. Our vocation is building commercial relationships to local and global level, providing through our practice areas a multidisciplinary portafolio which permits an integral analysis of business from our clients.

Mission

ESCOBAR RODRIGUEZ is engaged in to be an answer to the client’s needs, through an efficient process looking for optimize, prevent and legal risk mitigation as can be presented within your organization, both in operations developed and commercial relationships. 

Vision

In 2022, we will be leaders in the region with purpose of connection supply and demand from market into real sector, giving rise to innovation in insurance and legal audits, so that our clients have confidence and transparency in management board.

Services

We ofer services in legal audit and insurance.

Legal audit

This service looks for measure legal process efficiency, performing a complete analysis on the present status of the same and identifying important risk factors which may be against the interests of our clients. The outcome of the statutory audit includes the preparation of an improvement plan of the best optimization of the analyzed processes.
This kind of service, include the revision of the aspects, whom will be adjusted in accordance with your preferences:

  • Revision and adjustment of the commercial agreements and business cooperation
  • Revision and adjustment of administrative requirements.
  • Revision and adjustment of associated processes with corporate governance.
  • Revision and adjustment of internal recruitment processes and procedures. (contracting personnel and company work regulations).
  • Revision and diagnosis of the concurrent or future adversarial process.

Legal insurance

We provide the legal insurance counsel and legal consulting in decision making, and other requirements that compromise a company´s good functioning. Using this service, our client will get ensure timely and effective responses focuses in:

  • Attention of  PQR.
  • Attention of rights motion and constitutional actions.

  • Requirements of government and judicial authorities. 
  • All around your enterprise and on activities of the company.
